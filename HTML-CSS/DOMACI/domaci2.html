Porsche 992 GT3 RS

The 992 GT3 RS was announced in August 2022.

It features a dramatically improved aerodynamic profile compared to the 992 GT3, resulting in 860 kg of downforce at 285kmh, a two-fold increase over the 991.2 GT3 RS, and 409 kg of downforce at 200kmh.[56] The rear wing features a static portion and an active portion, which can open and close automatically based on vehicle data, or manually with a button fitted to the steering wheel.Its engine produces 525 PS at 8500 rpm and 465 Nm of torque at 6300 rpm.
